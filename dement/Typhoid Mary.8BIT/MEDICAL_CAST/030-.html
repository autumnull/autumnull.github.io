The Seventh or Facial Nerve

The facial nerve contains motor fibres only which supply the muscles of expression. It is accompanied by a small number of sensory fibres going to the external acoustic meatus, by fibres which excite salivary secretion and others which convey taste impulses from the anterior two thirds of the tongue.
