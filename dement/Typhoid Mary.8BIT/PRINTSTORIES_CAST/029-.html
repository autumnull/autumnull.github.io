TYPHOID MARY story 11

Typhoid Mary's new doctor rings her.
"You don't you really don't have typhoid Mary. Really truly. All the tests are back from the lab and it's good news Mary. You have a long life ahead of you girl. I'm happy for you. I'm glad."
"Thanks." says Typhoid Mary.
She hangs up.
"And so," she says, "And so I have a life ahead of me. Tomorrow and tomorrow and tomorrow evermore."
Typhoid Mary sits still with her future for a little while.
"No." says Typhoid Mary, "Ah shit. Not 50 more years. Not a long and productive life. Ah shit. Spare me from a long and productive life."
There is blackness.
