DIARY

Tues 7/5

Can't go to work. The feelings I feel that are love and desire are grief and amputation. I miss the one I love already. The separation comes with the meeting. It kills me. I know they will die soon. All my friends die. That's the way it goes.
This one that makes me feel so who is breath & touch & warmth to me and whom I know will die soon is the ones who are dead already who once were this one breath and warmth and touch and soon to die.
And this is not true. They aren't really all dead.
