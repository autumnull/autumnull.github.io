TYPHOID MARY story 7

"I wish they would let me do some therapy." says Typhoid Mary to the cuts on her wrists.
"Who are they that are stopping you?" ask the cuts.
"Fucked if I know." says Typhoid Mary.
"You could just kill yourself. It's easier. Why don't you just kill yourself Mary? Look at the blood." say the cuts.
"I have a great life. I'm a lucky person. I'm too lucky to kill myself. I carry with me the burden of the unlucky. I owe them and I owe them with my living, with time, with my old age and with my health and my effort and my guilt. I know I'm not allowed to kill myself."
"Heap of shit." say the cuts,"You don't owe them anything."
"I don't owe them anything?"
"Nothing."
"Then what am I doing alive?"
"Beats me."
