"...the sexual abuse of children is not an occasional deviant act, but a devastating commonplace fact of everyday life.
25% of rape victims are under 12 years.
70% of prostitutes and 80% of drug users were incest victims.
80% of juvenile offenders were sexually abused.

In a children's hospital in Washington, more injury and disease is caused by sexual abuse than there are broken bones and tonsillectomies put together. "

from THE BEST KEPT SECRET:SEXUAL ABUSE OF CHILDREN by Florence Rush 1980 reprinted by permission of the publisher, Prentice Hall / a division of Simon & Schuster, Englewood Cliffs, N.J. p xii
