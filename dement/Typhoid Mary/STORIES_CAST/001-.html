TYPHOID MARY story 13

Crying and very heavy in the body, Typhoid Mary goes to David Jones, to the perfume department.

"Oh this weight. This scream inside. These things that slow me down. Maybe I could lie down at the Dior counter and stop this life." she says.

At the Dior counter she buys a bottle of Poison for the Beautiful Lesbian. At the Chanel counter she buys a bottle of No.5 for Gabriel's Mother. She doesn't lie down.
