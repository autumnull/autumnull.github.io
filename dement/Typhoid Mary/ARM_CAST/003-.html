"Unproductive expenditures: luxury, mourning, war, cults, the construction of sumptuary monuments, games, spectacles, arts, perverse sexual activity ... in each case the accent is placed on a loss that must be as great as possible in order for that activity to to take on its true meaning."

Georges Bataille:VISIONS OF EXCESS: SELECTED WRITINGS 1927-1939, (trans & ed) Allan Stoekl, Theory and History of Literature, vol 14, University of Minnesota Press, Minneapolis, 1985, p118
