DIARY

Thurs 28/3

I lie in bed & think I could just reach over & pick up my blade & cut a long cut down from the wrist to the inside of the elbow and feel the blood rush relief like heroin vomit, like lying down after a hard day, like the stripjoint after an art dinner. The hatred washes out with the life .
I can feel the dizzy euphoria nausea of blood loss & drift off to blackness & peace after the sick spots in the vision pass and die softly die blood on the white linen sheets. But I don't.
