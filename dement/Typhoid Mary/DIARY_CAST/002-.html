DIARY

Sun 31/3

Read Lyotard & watch TV. I think I could swallow all the anti- depressants in the cupboard and would it kill me? I don't know what pills can kill. I remember once I took 19 phenobarbs and scotch and it didn't even touch the sides and the three days unconscious after barbiturates and retching in the bath at some stage.
I read Anna Kavan & listen to the prisoners request show. J plays Isaac Hayes for me and I am happy for those minutes.
