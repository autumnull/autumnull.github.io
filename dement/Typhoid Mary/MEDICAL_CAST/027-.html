The First or Olfactory Nerve

The filaments of the olfactory nerve carry impulses from the smell receptors in the nasalmucosa to the olfactory bulb, from which the olfactory tract conveys them along the floor of the anterior fossa of the skull to the olfactory area of the cerebral cortex in the neighbourhood of uncus.
