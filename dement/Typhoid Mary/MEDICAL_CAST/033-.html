ABRIDGED PRODUCT INFORMATION
SEREPAX (OXAZEPAM)
INDICATIONS: Anxiety, agitation, tension, irritability, alcoholic withdrawal syndrome. CONTRAINDICATIONS: Hypersensitivity to benzodiazepines, children under 6 years, early pregnancy. PRECAUTIONS: Periodic blood counts and liver function tests during long term therapy. Late stage pregnancy and during lactation. When therapy is no longer required, decrease dosage gradually. Use of benzodiazepines may lead to dependence. WARNING: Patients should be advised not to operate machinery or motor vehicles until it is known that they do not become drowsy or dizzy. The need for continued therapy should be periodically determined ADVERSE REACTIONS: Transient mild drowsiness early in therapy. INTERACTIONS: CNS depressants and alcohol potentiate benzodiazepine sedative effects.
