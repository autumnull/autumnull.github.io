"The body's inside... shows up in order to compensate for the collapse of the border between inside and outside. It is as if the skin... no longer guaranteed the integrity of one's 'own and clean self' but, scraped or transparent, invisible or taut, gave way before the abjection of its contents."

Julia Kristeva: POWERS OF HORROR trans Leon Roudiez, Columbia University Press, NY, 1987, p53
