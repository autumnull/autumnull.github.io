^ back to break: a hypertext exercise in hyperempathy

main deck

it’s dark and raining on the main deck. the wind is strong, but not storming – about a 5 on the beaufort scale. the botswain shouts orders to crewmen as they busy themselves with ropes and chains and wheels.

in the middle of the deck, a great wooden mast supports the sails. at the stern end, the captain’s quarters, and stairs leading down to the lower deck.

  • climb the mast
  • knock on captain’s door
  • descend to the lower deck
