^ back to break: a hypertext exercise in hyperempathy

invitation

“i also think the transexual reading of prometheus ligns up with my approach to being trans in general, in that like, it’s not moving from one gender to another, it’s a resistance to the order of things, whether that’s natural or social or, uh, ontological? idk if that’s the right word but i mean uh… pretty much what you said, like, an inclination to do and become things you’re not supposed to, while you can. oh btw, uh, speaking of, are you doing anything the rest of the evening?”

“no, how come?”

she talks as her eyes scan her room, grabbing a few items, “i found this place, next to the park. i wana go check it out. you’ll like it.”

“it’s raining”

“bring a coat? probably also some grippy boots, maybe an energy bar or so”

“wait am i gona have to climb?”

“only a little, just one fence, it’s not spiked or anything”

“uh… okay, sure.”

“i’ll come by yours at 11”

you eye her suspiciously for a few moments, trying your best to think ahead.

“…are we gona break the law?”
