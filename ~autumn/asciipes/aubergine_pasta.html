aubergine pasta

serves 4

cherry tomato 250g
olive oil
red onion 1
garlic 2
red chilli 1
aubergine 1
tinned tomatoes 400g
oregano 1tbsp
salt + black pepper            _.
                            _-" *\
                         _-"\*_-'"
                __     ."   _\
        ~c==.  ((@)  <`_,.-'
       ====================

finely chop red onion, garlic, chilli
cut aubergine into quartered 1/2 inch disks

fry tomatoes 4-5 minutes
set aside, put pasta on
fry red onion, garlic, chilli in olive oil 2-3 minutes
add aubergine
fry 1-2 minutes each side
add canned tomatoes, boil, simmer for 2-3 minutes
add tomatoes, oregano, cook 1 minute
add salt, pepper
