banana bread

     I
    ( ".___.--#
     '-.___.-"

bananas (speckled) 3
wet:
 mild oil 1/3cup
 vanilla extract 1tsp
dry:
 wholewheat flour 1and3/4cup
 white sugar 1/3cup
 baking powder 1tsp
 baking soda 1tsp
 salt 1/8tsp

preheat oven to 180C
grease a loaf tin (3x7inch works pretty good)

peel and mash bananas in a large mixing bowl
add wet
mix pretty thoroughly, so that some air bubbles are visible
add dry
mix only until powder is gone, then stop
pour the mixture into the loaf tin
bake for about 45-50 minutes

when done, keep wrapped in cling film in the fridge
