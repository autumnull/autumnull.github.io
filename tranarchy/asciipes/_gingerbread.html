gingerbread

all purpose flour 2 cups
baking powder 2 tsp
baking soda 1/2 tsp
ground ginger 2 tsp
cinnamon 1 tsp
brown sugar 1/4 cup
granulated sugar 1/4 cup
unsalted butter (softened) 1/2 cup
large egg 1
milk 2/3 cup
unsulfured molasses 2/3 cup

grease 9x5 inch loaf pan
preheat oven to 180C

bowl 1:
combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, ginger & cinnamon

bowl 2:
combine the milk & molasses

bowl 3:
beat butter & sugars until light & fluffy
beat in the egg
alternate adding the mixtures from bowls 1&2 (fold together w/ rubber spatula)
pour into the loaf pan
bake for ~50 minutes or a bit less for more battery gingerbread
