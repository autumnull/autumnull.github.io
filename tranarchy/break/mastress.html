the information comes flooding into you, filling your cache almost instantly, sending fizzing sensations across your disk. it tastes salty and ionic for a few moments, and you barely have time to wonder if one of your capacitors has burst before you receive the sleep command, hypnotising, your screen dims, plays a brief shutdown animation, and then turns dark.
