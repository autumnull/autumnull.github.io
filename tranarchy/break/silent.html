^ back to break: a hypertext exercise in hyperempathy

boy

the voice lumbers down the stairs.

“ah there y’are, what’s got into you? a cabin-boy’s no use if he won’t come when called!”

confused and indignant, you pipe up, “but, i’m not a boy? you must be looking for someone else”

the figure scoffs derisively. “yeah, right, my sincerest apologies to the Big Strong Man dawdling in his hammock all day!” he grabs a fistful of your shirt, lifting you into the air and pinning your back against a nearby post almost effortlessly. you pray he hasn’t noticed your bindings, intimately aware that this man’s could crush you into greasy scrap without a second thought.

“yer a boy until ye can keep both feet on the ground around the real men. understand?”

  • “aye, sir”
