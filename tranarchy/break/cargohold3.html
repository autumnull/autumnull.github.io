^ back to break: a hypertext exercise in hyperempathy

cargo hold

the cargo hold carries food stores for the voyage, medical supplies, and the carpenter’s tools. it’s cramped and poorly lit with glowing vacuum tubes, and the air is heavy with the smell of different resins, gums and greases.

from the rafters, above a crate of assorted power adapters, hangs your hammock. a staircase at the stern leads up to the lower deck.

  • ascend the stairs
