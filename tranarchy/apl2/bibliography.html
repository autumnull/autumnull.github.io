This bibliography contains selected titles on APL2 and works cited in the text. It does not contain historical references or references to other implementations of APL.

  • Brown, James A., “Inside the APL2 workspace,” APL Quote Quad, Vol. 15, No. 4 (1985).

  • Brown, James A., Edward V. Eusebi, Janice Cook,and Leo Groner, “Algorithms for Artificial Intelligence in APL2,” IBM Santa Teresa Technical Report (TR 03.281) (May 1986).

  • Eusebi, Edward V., “Operators for Recursion,” APL Quote Quad, Vol. 15, No. 4 (1985).

  • Mayhew, Loren B., “Increasing Productivity with ISPF/APL2,” APL Quote Quad, Vol. 16, No. 4 (1986).
  • Polivka, Raymond P., “The Impact of APL2 on Teaching APL,” APL Quote Quad, Vol. 14, No. 4 (1984)
  • Polivka, Raymond P., “APL2—An Introduction (Concepts and Principles)” APL Quote Quad Vol. 16, No. 4, (1986)
  • Thomson, Norman, “APL2—A Mathematician’s Delight,” APL Quote Quad, Vol. 16, No. 4 (1986).

  • Vilenkin, N. Ya, “Combinatorics,” Academic Press, 1971.
